About the Company


We are travel agency company that is based in Toronto, Ontario and are focused on creating the most unique and memorable experiences for clients, We currently offer trips all over the globe

Dream Escape is a travel agency that understands the importance of creating customized travel experiences. They take the time to get to know each customer and their unique preferences, so they can create travel plans that are tailored to their needs. Whether a customer is interested in adventure travel, cultural experiences, or simply relaxing on a beach, Dream Escape can create an itinerary that matches their desires. One of the agency's strengths is its team of travel planners, who are experts in various destinations around the world. They have first-hand knowledge of different cultures, languages, and customs, which enables them to craft itineraries that provide a truly immersive experience. Furthermore, they use their network of trusted partners and vendors to ensure that customers receive the best possible service and value for their money.

Dream Escape also offers a range of travel services to make the planning process as easy as possible. They can handle everything from booking flights and accommodations to arranging transportation and local tours. In addition, they provide ongoing support throughout the trip to ensure that customers have a stress-free and enjoyable experience.

Overall, Dream Escape is a travel agency that stands out for its dedication to creating personalized travel experiences that match each customer's unique interests and preferences. With their expert team of travel planners, wide range of services, and commitment to customer satisfaction, it's no wonder that customers rave about their experiences with this agency
